## Description

Canilendar is the planner built for professional dog walkers who need every pickup, route, and free slot under control.

When your day is packed with dogs, clients, timings, and repeat walks, small mistakes get expensive. A missed pickup, an overbooked afternoon, or a forgotten reminder can throw off your entire schedule. Canilendar helps you stay one step ahead.

Plan every dog pickup in one clear place. See your day at a glance. Know instantly how many spots are still available. Catch full-capacity days before you promise one walk too many. And get reminders before appointments so nothing slips through the cracks.

Canilendar is designed for real commercial dog-walking work:

Track daily dog pickups and appointments
Get reminders before scheduled walks
See remaining capacity for each day
Spot fully booked days immediately
Reuse dog details to schedule faster
Manage recurring appointments with less admin
Keep your working day calm, clear, and organized
Instead of juggling messages, memory, and messy calendars, Canilendar gives you a cleaner way to run your business. You spend less time checking availability and more time walking dogs, serving clients, and growing your schedule with confidence.

If you walk dogs professionally, Canilendar helps you stay organized, avoid overbooking, and keep every day moving smoothly.
